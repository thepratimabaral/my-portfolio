About

A data‑driven UX and ICT specialist passionate about creating inclusive, user‑centric products, particularly in emerging markets and under‑served communities.

I’ve spent my career working at the intersection of technology, agriculture, and education – designing, implementing, and evaluating digital tools that make complex information accessible to farmers, students, and stakeholders.

My work ranges from building end‑to‑end data pipelines and predictive models, to leading usability testing, running randomized control trials, and translating findings into practical product decisions.

I’m currently studying MSc Big Data and Data Science Technology at Northumbria University in London, building deeper skills in machine learning, analytics, and modern data tooling to strengthen my impact as a researcher and product partner.

In the future, I’m planning to use this space as a blog to share case studies, field learnings, and reflections on building human‑centred data products in the real world.
